About the Fund

The Lancaster Stands Up Immigration Working Group, in partnership with CIRAC, Church World Services, and other local immigration groups, has set up a fund to provide direct financial support to immigrants who are vulnerable to deportation from Lancaster County.

When the fund is up and running, we will provide small grants of $2000 to families suffering from hardship related to deportation proceedings in Lancaster County.

  • The number of people detained by ice is increasing.
  • Ice Activity increased 60% in Lancaster City just this year.
  • 7,700 children in PA have been detained by ICE.

How can I apply?

On August 1, 2018 we will accept applications on a rolling basis. Preference will given to applicants who meet these four criteria:

  1. Referred from a non-profit organization working with detainees (CWS, PIRC, PICC), church, or an immigration lawyer in Lancaster County.
  2. Detained and/or vulnerable to deportation.
  3. Demonstrates financial need.
  4. Lives in Lancaster County.
