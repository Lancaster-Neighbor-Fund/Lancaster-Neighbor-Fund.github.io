Lancaster Neighbor Fund COVID-19 Security Net

The Lancaster Neighbor Fund has established an emergency fund for our undocumented neighbors, many of whom work in industries that do not offer health benefits or PTO. Many have jobs that are closing their doors but folks are not able to apply for unemployment benefits (or any benefits) without legal status. We know that many immigrant families were excluded from stimulus relief as well. Money collected here will be used to give direct cash aid to our undocumented neighbors. Thank you for your generous contributions.
